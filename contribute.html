Collaborations and contributions are welcome!
There are many ways to get involved with the rOpenSci project. We aim to extend the approach to international level and to further statistical programming languages. We are actively seeking partners in other countries. Join the movement, and let us know of related initiatives.
  • Keep up with latest updates on twitter
  • Link to us from your blog
  • Collaborate with us on the different packages via github.
  • Get in touch if you would like to suggest or collaborate with us on a new R package.
  • Help organize a rOpenGov workshop at your institution.
