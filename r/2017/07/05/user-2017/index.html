Presenting at useR2017 in Brussels

Annual useR-conference is organised this year in Brussels, Belgium. rOpenGov will be presenting in two occasion on Thursday 6th. Leo Lahti will give an general overview of rOpenGov in his lightning talk rOpenGov - Community project for open government data at 17.50 in room 3.02. At 13.30 in Plenary room Markus Kainu will speak about Community-based learning and knowledge sharing - Teaching R withing organisation using edu -package.
