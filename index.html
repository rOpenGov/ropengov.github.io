Welcome to rOpenGov

An avalanche of open data on elections, economics, demography, traffic, and other fields relevant to computational social science have become available. New algorithms are needed to take full advantage of these new information resources, and to access, analyse and communicate such information in a fully transparent and reproducible manner. A community-driven software ecosystem dedicated to social and political sciences can provide a solution and revolutionize the field as already happened in biological sciences during the last decade.

We are now building a community of independent developers around open goverment data analytics. Hence, we are calling for contributions in the form of R packages. By joining the community you will get added visibility and recognition for your project and an opportunity to share ideas, attract contributors, provide resources and pool the efforts with others working on related topics. Here we build on lessons learned from analogous projects in other fields such as Bioconductor. If you are writing related software on other languages than R, let us know. Don't hesitate to contact us!
