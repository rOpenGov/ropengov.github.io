--- layout: page root: true title: tagline: rOpenGov version: 0.2 ghurl: https://github.com/ropengov language: en --- {% include JB/setup %} R Ecosystem for Open Government Data and Computational Social Science

An avalanche of open data on elections, economics, demography, traffic, and other fields relevant to computational social science has become available. New algorithms are needed to take advantage of the new information resources. A community-driven software ecosystem dedicated to social and political sciences will provide a solution and has a potential to revolutionize the field by providing transparent and reproducible tools to access, analyse and communicate information, as already happened for biological sciences during the last decade.

Join us! We are now building a community of independent developers around open goverment data analytics (see the projects page). We welcome new projects and would love to learn more about your work on related topics in R or other languages. You will get added visibility and recognition, an opportunity to share ideas, attract contributors, and pool the efforts. Don't hesitate to contact us!
