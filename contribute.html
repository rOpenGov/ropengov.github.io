Join the community

rOpenGov is a loose community of R package developers who like to work on joint projects related to open government data, computational social science and digital humanities. You are welcome join us at IRC (ropengov@Freenode) and Google+, or contact us if you like to contribute an R package or blog post. All feedback, bug reports and support are very welcome. In return you may increase the visibility of your project and connect with other developers with related interested.
