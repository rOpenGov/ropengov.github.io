Join the community

rOpenGov is a community-driven ecosystem of R packages from independent developers related to open government data and computational social sciences. We promote online documentation and use of the tools, and support package co-operability. We encourage contributions:

Contribute code By contributing an R package you can take advantage of our automated utilities, increase the visibility of your project, and connect with other developers. Just let us know the Github URL of your package, and we will add in on the list of contributing packages.

Test individual packages and report issues at their Github pages

Give feedback Suggest new data sources where R tools are needed, and give general feedback on the project through the mailing list and other forums
